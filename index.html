Village of 45 Kasbahs

Discover the
Soul of Nkob

The village of 45 Kasbahs awaits. Immerse yourself in the timeless beauty of Berber architecture, earthy colors, and the warm hospitality of the Draa Valley.

Heritage & Traditions

The Ait Atta Legacy

Architecture of Earth

Nkob is unique in Morocco, boasting 45 Kasbahs built entirely from rammed earth and straw. This architectural style, inherited from our ancestors, keeps the interiors cool in summer and warm in winter. Walking through our streets is like walking through a living open-air museum.

Berber Hospitality

In the Ait Atta tribe, a guest is a gift from God. The ceremony of Mint Tea is sacred. It is not just a drink, but a moment of sharing, prepared with fresh mint from the oasis and served with precision. Here, we take the time to connect.

Arts & Traditions

Nkob is famous for its intricate Henna designs, a symbol of joy and protection used during weddings and celebrations. Our rugs, woven by hand, tell the stories of the Atlas Mountains through geometric patterns and vibrant natural colors.
